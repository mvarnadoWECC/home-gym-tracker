One-time setup

Sync needs a tiny free backend. The backends/ folder has three you can deploy with an account you already have — Deno Deploy or Netlify (both sign in with GitHub), or Cloudflare. Deploy one, paste its URL into js/config.js, and this panel switches on. See backends/README.md — Claude Code can do the deploy for you.

Safety net

Backup

Storage can occasionally fail. Tap Copy backup anytime to grab all your data as text — paste it somewhere safe (Notes, email). To restore on a new device, paste it below and tap Restore.
